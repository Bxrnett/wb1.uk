About WB1 - Staffordshire Web Design

Our Mission

WB1 empowers construction companies, builders, and contractors with professional websites that generate leads and win projects. Based in Staffordshire, we specialize in creating digital solutions that help construction businesses build trust, showcase their work, and grow their client pipeline.

Our Approach

We believe in creating digital solutions that are not just beautiful, but effective. Every landing page, website, and tool we build is designed with a clear purpose: to generate leads and drive growth. We focus on conversion, not just aesthetics.
